Projects

  • We proudly worked on a project delivering essential food products and other supplies to Lusaka City Mission District during their community outreach program. As they were camping in the outskirts of Central Mungule, we ensured timely delivery of fresh produce, water, and essential goods to support their efforts. This initiative not only strengthened our logistical capabilities but also deepened our connection with local communities, helping to provide nutritious meals and vital supplies to those in need. It was a humbling experience that showcased the power of teamwork and our commitment to making a positive impact.
  • We collaborated with local NGOs to deliver market-fresh food products to vulnerable communities in Kalikiliki. Our team organized weekly deliveries of fresh fruits, vegetables, and grains, supporting hundreds of households who had limited access to nutritious food. This project was instrumental in alleviating food insecurity and fostering community resilience.
  • We partnered with local authorities to deliver fresh food supplies to rural schools in Chibombo, ensuring students received healthy meals during a critical school feeding program. Despite the remote locations and challenging conditions, our team successfully coordinated logistics to provide fruits, vegetables, and grains, supporting over 500 students for three months.
  • During the COVID-19 pandemic, we launched a project to deliver fresh meals and protective equipment to health workers stationed at Makeni Field Hospital. Our team provided daily deliveries of nutritious meals, snacks, and beverages to boost morale and energy levels, while also donating hand sanitizers and masks to help safeguard their health.
  • In response to the flooding in Kafue in 2023, we quickly mobilized to deliver emergency food supplies and hygiene products to displaced families. Collaborating with disaster relief teams, we ensured that affected households had access to essential provisions like bread, canned goods, and drinking water, making a meaningful difference during a difficult time.
